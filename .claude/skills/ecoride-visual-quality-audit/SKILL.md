# ECO RIDE Visual Quality Audit

## Mission

Review the UI before client presentation.

The goal is to ensure the project visually matches the expected ECO RIDE direction.

## Audit categories

### 1. Brand feeling

Check:

- Does it feel premium?
- Does it feel automotive?
- Does it feel trustworthy?
- Does it feel modern and ecological?
- Does it avoid looking generic?

### 2. Mobile app quality

Check:

- Is bottom navigation clean?
- Are CTAs thumb-friendly?
- Are cards readable?
- Are filters usable?
- Does the site feel like an app on mobile?

### 3. Cinematic impact

Check:

- Does the homepage have a strong hero?
- Are large visuals used well?
- Is the typography strong?
- Are dark sections elegant?
- Is the storytelling clear?

### 4. Catalogue usability

Check:

- Can users filter easily?
- Are prices visible?
- Is availability visible?
- Are vehicle cards scannable?
- Does desktop use space well?

### 5. Vehicle detail conversion

Check:

- Is the vehicle image impressive?
- Are specs clear?
- Is the CTA visible?
- Are services and conditions clear?
- Is trust strong enough?

### 6. Consistency

Check:

- Same radius system?
- Same color palette?
- Same button style?
- Same card style?
- Same spacing rhythm?
- Same icon style?

### 7. Legal/design safety

Check:

- No unlicensed car brand logos.
- No fake certifications.
- No fake guarantees.
- Legal pages are placeholders.
- Commercial claims are realistic.

## Acceptance criteria

The prototype is ready if:

- Homepage impresses.
- Mobile feels app-like.
- Catalogue is useful.
- Detail page converts.
- Admin demo shows business value.
- No obvious visual inconsistency remains.
