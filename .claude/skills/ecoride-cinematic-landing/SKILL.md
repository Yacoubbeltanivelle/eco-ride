# ECO RIDE Cinematic Landing

## Mission

Create the premium landing page identity for ECO RIDE.

The landing must feel:

- cinematic;
- high-end automotive;
- trustworthy;
- ecological;
- conversion-focused.

## Homepage structure

Build the homepage with these sections:

1. Cinematic Hero
2. Three offer paths
3. Featured vehicles
4. Why ECO RIDE
5. Location VTC section
6. Achat occasion section
7. Mandataire auto section
8. Process in 3 steps
9. Trust / reviews
10. FAQ
11. Final CTA
12. Footer

## Hero section

Hero mood:

- dark background;
- large vehicle image;
- soft glow;
- editorial grid lines;
- big title;
- compact supporting text;
- primary CTA;
- secondary CTA.

Suggested copy direction:

```
Location, vente et recherche automobile.
Une mobilité plus simple, plus fiable, plus responsable.
```

CTA:

- Voir les véhicules
- Demander une proposition

## Editorial grid

Use thin lines and structured blocks.

Examples:

- label at top left;
- title large on right;
- specs row;
- image below;
- small metadata blocks.

Do not make it too abstract. ECO RIDE must remain commercial and clear.

## Offer cards

Create 3 large cards:

### Location VTC

For drivers, independents and companies.

CTA:

- Louer un véhicule

### Achat occasion

Reliable, checked and ready-to-drive vehicles.

CTA:

- Acheter un véhicule

### Mandataire auto

Personalized sourcing for recent vehicles.

CTA:

- Trouver mon véhicule

## Premium storytelling sections

Use long-form visual sections inspired by electric vehicle landing pages.

Possible sections:

- "Sélectionné avec rigueur"
- "Prêt à rouler"
- "Pensé pour les professionnels"
- "Une mobilité plus responsable"

Each section should include:

- large visual;
- concise title;
- 2–3 proof points;
- CTA or internal link.

## Trust elements

Add:

- véhicules contrôlés;
- démarches transparentes;
- accompagnement personnalisé;
- assistance selon formule;
- prix clairs;
- contact direct.

## Desktop style

Use:

- large type;
- black background;
- wide sections;
- visual contrast;
- subtle gradients;
- refined whitespace.

## Mobile adaptation

On mobile:

- reduce hero height;
- keep title punchy;
- stack cards;
- use horizontal carousels;
- keep CTA sticky.

## Avoid

- Making it look like a generic SaaS page.
- Too much abstract AI-style design.
- Hiding the actual offer.
- Long paragraphs.
- Fake claims.
- Fake certifications.

## Acceptance criteria

The landing is successful if:

- The user understands ECO RIDE in less than 10 seconds.
- The page feels premium and automotive.
- Location, vente and mandataire are immediately clear.
- The mobile hero is impressive.
- The CTA is obvious.
